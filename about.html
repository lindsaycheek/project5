Ember Rising is a professional artist and entertainer based in Asheville, NC. Ember’s vibrant presence and unique style never fail to captivate an audience. Truly a natural born performer, her excitement is contagious. She specializes in fire dancing, hula hooping, LED spinning, art installations, and costume design.

Ember started dance training at a very young age, focusing on modern and world styles. In 2011, she fell in love with hula hooping at a music festival, diving head first into prop manipulation. Continually pushing herself as a performer, she’s been practicing fire manipulation for over seven years now. When Ember isn’t entertaining audiences by playing with fire, she enjoys giving private lessons and creating one of a kind costumes.

Ember Rising has graced stages across the country. Whether it’s a private party, music event, or arts festival, Ember is sure to create an experience to remember.
